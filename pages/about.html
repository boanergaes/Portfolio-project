BIO

I grew up in Debre Markos, East Gojjam, and attended three different schools to complete my elementary and high school education. Interestingly, I had no interest in programming while growing up—in fact, I wrote my first "Hello, World!" program only after joining Engineering at Addis Ababa University.

I always wanted to join Medical school as a kid and even came to Addis Ababa University hopping to join Tkur Anbessa Med School. Fortunately, I didn’t pass the entrance exam—and instead found myself in the Engineering department. Getting into Software Engineering at AAU wasn’t easy, but by God’s grace, I got it.

Now, I’m on the path to becoming a Software Engineer. Now that I have been coding for some time, I’ve come to see programming as an interesting and challenging craft. It's worth every time and effort put on it. I'm currently focused on web development and it's going great so far. With God's help, I aim to make the most of the opportunity I’ve been given.
